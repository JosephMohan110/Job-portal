Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about WorkConnect

How do I create an account on WorkConnect?

Creating an account is simple. Click on the "Register" button in the top right corner, choose whether you're a worker or employer, fill in your details, and verify your email address. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

How does WorkConnect ensure the safety of workers and employers?

We have multiple safety measures including identity verification, user ratings and reviews, secure payment processing, and a dedicated support team available 24/7 to handle any issues. All users must agree to our community guidelines.

What types of jobs can I find on WorkConnect?

WorkConnect features a wide range of daily wage jobs including construction work, plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, painting, cleaning services, delivery work, agricultural labor, and many other skilled and unskilled positions.

How does the payment system work?

WorkConnect offers multiple payment options. Employers can pay workers directly through our secure platform using UPI, bank transfer, or cash. We also provide payment protection to ensure workers receive their agreed-upon wages.

Can I use WorkConnect in multiple cities?

Yes, WorkConnect is available in over 200 cities across India. You can update your location preferences in your account settings to find work opportunities or workers in different cities.
